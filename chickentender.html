Frustrated with trying to decide where you are going to eat tonight? "I don't care, what do you want?" not working for you? Try Chicken Tender, a matching app for food! In just a few minutes, the app will guide you to making a decision between a few great options near you! Save your marriage today by installing Chicken Tender!

This app was made primarily using Javascript, CSS, and JQuery.
